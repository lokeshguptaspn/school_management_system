About Canteen

The school canteen is one of the liveliest areas of the campus. It is spread over 375 metrs and accommodates about 150 users. Staff members and the students share the canteen with separate and common sitting areas. Our canteen aims at providing a variety of quality food & beverages at highly competitive rates. A multi-product retail outlet of Nestle is also functional adjacent to the main canteen building. The overall administration of the Canteen is under the supervision of a Canteen Committee drawn by the school Staff Council.
